About Njala University

Njala University is a public university located in Bo, Sierra Leone. Established in 1964, it is one of the oldest universities in the country. Njala University is known for its strong programs in agriculture, forestry, and environmental sciences.

Why Choose Njala University?

Njala University provides a comprehensive educational experience, combining strong academic programs, experienced faculty, research opportunities, a beautiful campus, and affordable tuition. Students can benefit from the university's focus on agriculture, forestry, and environmental sciences, while also enjoying the advantages of modern facilities and a supportive learning environment.
