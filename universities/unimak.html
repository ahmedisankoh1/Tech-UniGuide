About University of Makeni

The University of Makeni is a public university located in Makeni, Sierra Leone. Established in 2005, it is a relatively new institution but has rapidly gained recognition for its academic programs and commitment to quality education. The university offers a diverse range of programs across various disciplines.

Why Choose University of Makeni

The University of Makeni offers a vibrant and stimulating learning environment with a focus on academic excellence and student success. Students can benefit from the university's diverse range of programs, experienced faculty, modern facilities, and affordable tuition. The university's commitment to quality education and its location in the heart of Makeni make it an attractive choice for students seeking a rewarding academic experience.
